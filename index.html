PREPATATION TIPS

1. Plan: Create a study schedule covering all subjects.
2. Practice: Solve previous papers and take mock tests.
3. Understand: Focus on understanding concepts over memorization.
4. Revise: Regularly revise to reinforce learning.
5. Resources: Use quality textbooks and online materials.
6. Seek Help: Clarify doubts from mentors or online forums.
7. Stay Healthy: Prioritize sleep, nutrition, and exercise.
8. Stay Positive: Believe in yourself and stay motivated.
